# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple expectation: be respectful, constructive, and welcoming.

## Expected behavior

Be kind in discussion, give and receive feedback thoughtfully, and focus on the work rather than the person.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, and other conduct that makes collaboration unsafe are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, pause participation, or take other reasonable actions to keep the community safe.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness misconduct, contact the maintainers privately.
